Ascend – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 May 2025

1. Overview

Ascend (“the app”) is a personal productivity and habit‑tracking application created by Andika Yudhatrisna. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you give us. This document explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Email Address – used solely to authenticate you and restore your data on other devices.
  • User ID – a random identifier generated by Firebase Authentication; used internally to link your schedules and habits to your account.
  • Habit & Schedule Data – titles, notes, completion records, timer logs. These are created by you to make the app work.
  • Crash & Performance Logs – automatic, anonymized diagnostics supplied by Apple & Firebase Crashlytics to keep the app stable.

We do not collect sensitive personal information, location, contacts, or advertising identifiers. We do not sell your data or share it with third‑party marketers.

3. How We Use the Data

  • App Functionality – saving and syncing your habits, schedules, and progress across devices.
  • Analytics – aggregate, de‑identified statistics that show which screens crash or how often features are used. This helps us prioritize fixes and improvements.
  • Customer Support – if you email us for help, we may access your anonymized user ID to diagnose the problem.

4. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored in Google Firebase (United States) using industry‑standard encryption at rest and in transit. Only the account owner can read their habit data. We maintain strict access controls and regularly update dependencies to patch security issues.

5. Your Choices

  • You can export or delete all of your data from the app at any time via Settings › Delete Account.
  • Deleting your account removes your email and all associated habit data from our servers within 30 days.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. When we do, we’ll post the revised version here and update the “Effective date.”

7. Contact

If you have questions about privacy or data protection, please email support@ascend‑app.com.
